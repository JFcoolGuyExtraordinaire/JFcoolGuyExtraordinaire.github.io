Training Procedure

This procedure is to be followed by the trainer while instructing new technicians.

1.) Instruct the new technician on procedures. You may use this website or the help desk folder.

2.) Show new technicians where all bins and loaners are located. If you do not know where something is, ask.

3.) Train new tecnicians on the usage of Trello

4.) Explain the bulletin board to new technicians. Yellow cards are tasks for technicians.

Extras

If you have to leave the help desk to complete a task, make sure to take the badge located by the bulletin board.

Make sure to check the bulletin board each day for tasks. If an issue arises and you are not immediately working on it, make a card.

Cards in the done section should be removed every Friday
